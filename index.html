Government Job Announcements

Unofficial App Information

Our Mission

The "Government Job Announcements" app aims to assist job seekers by compiling and displaying job vacancies advertised by government units in an easy-to-access format.

What the App Offers

Facilitated Access

We publish and display advertised jobs, including the required conditions stated in the original advertisements, as well as the dates of electronic and oral exams (according to available information).

Verified Data Source

All job data is compiled from the **official government jobs portal** of the **Central Agency for Organization and Administration in the Arab Republic of Egypt**.

To view the official source and apply for jobs, please visit:

jobs.caoa.gov.eg

Important Disclaimer

Independence: This application (Government Job Announcements) is an independent application and **does not represent** the Central Agency for Organization and Administration or any other governmental entity in Egypt, nor does it claim any governmental affiliation.

Authority: The application is not affiliated with any governmental institution and **does not have the authority** to hire, receive, or process applications.

User Responsibility: The information provided here is for informational purposes only. Users should **always refer to the official website** mentioned above to apply for jobs, verify the accuracy of any information, or obtain final and official details.
